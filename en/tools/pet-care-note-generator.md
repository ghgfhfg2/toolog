## Why use a pet care note?
When someone else watches your pet, small routines can be easy to miss: meal amounts, walk timing, litter or potty checks, medicine, dislikes, and emergency contacts.

This generator turns those details into a clear handoff note you can copy into a chat or document. It does not provide veterinary advice; it only organizes the information you enter.

## How to use it
1. Enter the pet name, species, and personality notes.
2. Add meals, walks or play, potty/litter details, medicine, cautions, and contacts.
3. Click `Generate note`.
4. Review the result and copy it for the sitter or family member.

## Good use cases
- A family member is helping for one day.
- A sitter is meeting your pet for the first time.
- Medicine or special caution needs to stand out.

## FAQ
### Does this tool judge my pet’s health?
No. If you notice concerning symptoms, contact a veterinarian.

### Is this a replacement for a sitter agreement?
No. Use it as a care guide only. Fees, access, liability, and emergencies should be agreed separately.

### How do I keep the note short?
Put immediate actions first and keep background notes brief.
