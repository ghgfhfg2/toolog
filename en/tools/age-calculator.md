## See all key age details at once
Need a reliable age check for forms, healthcare, school eligibility, or hiring paperwork?
This tool shows everything in one view:
- Exact age (international standard)
- Korean counting age (optional)
- Total months and days lived
- Days left until next birthday

## Key features
- **Automatic exact-age calculation** based on whether your birthday has passed
- **Optional Korean counting age** display with toggle
- **Lifetime duration view** in months and days
- **Birthday countdown** with the next birthday date
- **Input validation** for invalid date combinations

## How to use
1. Enter your date of birth.
2. Keep today as reference date or choose a custom date.
3. Turn Korean counting age display on/off if needed.
4. Check the result cards and copy summary if you want to share it.

## FAQ
### Can I use this for future planning dates?
Yes. You can set any future date to preview expected age values.

### Is Korean counting age mandatory?
No. It is optional and can be hidden.

### Does this replace official legal age checks?
No. It is a practical calculator. For legal procedures, always follow the official authority’s rule.
