## Get age details in one screen
Need a quick and reliable age check for forms, healthcare, or eligibility?
This tool shows:
- Exact age (international standard)
- Korean counting age (optional)
- Total months and days lived
- Days until next birthday

## How to use
1. Enter date of birth.
2. Keep today or choose a custom reference date.
3. Review age summary cards instantly.
