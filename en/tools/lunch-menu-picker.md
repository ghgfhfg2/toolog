## Why this lunch menu picker helps
Sometimes the hardest part of a meal is not paying for it — it is deciding what to eat.
This tool reduces decision fatigue by narrowing your options to three practical picks.

## What it considers
- meal time
- eating alone or with others
- budget range
- current mood
- whether you want hot dishes or rice-based dishes included

## Typical use cases
### Solo lunch
Useful when you need something quick and do not want to overthink it.

### Team lunch
Helpful when everyone says “anything is fine” and nobody actually chooses.

### Dinner indecision
Works just as well when you want a few realistic dinner ideas after work.
