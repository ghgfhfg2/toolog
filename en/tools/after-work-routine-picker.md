## Why use this picker?
After work, time is limited and the options are endless. Exercise, chores, study, hobbies, or real rest can all sound reasonable, and deciding can drain the evening.

This picker uses your remaining time, energy, must-do tasks, and mood to suggest a routine you can realistically start tonight.

## How to use it
1. Choose how much free time you have left.
2. Set your energy level and current mood.
3. Add must-do tasks, one per line.
4. Choose tonight’s main goal and run the picker.
5. Copy the suggested 30–90 minute routine into your notes.
