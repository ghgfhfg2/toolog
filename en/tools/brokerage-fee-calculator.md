## What this tool does
This calculator estimates the **maximum legal brokerage fee** for Korean real estate transactions.

It supports:
- Home sale
- Jeonse lease
- Monthly rent lease (Wolse)

For monthly rent, transaction value is converted by:
**deposit + monthly rent × 100**.

## Key features
- Transaction-type based rate brackets
- Maximum cap calculation by legal range
- VAT-included reference amount
- Rent conversion helper for deposit/monthly rent inputs

## How to use
1. Choose transaction type (sale/jeonse/wolse).
2. Enter transaction amount (or deposit + monthly rent for wolse).
3. Check estimated max brokerage fee and VAT reference.
