## Calculate weekly holiday pay in seconds
Weekly holiday pay can be confusing for hourly and part-time workers.
This calculator quickly shows **eligibility + weekly/monthly estimate** from simple inputs.

## Key features
- **Automatic eligibility check** (15+ contracted weekly hours)
- **Holiday hour auto-calculation** (weekly hours ÷ workdays)
- **Legal cap handling** (max 8 holiday hours)
- **Weekly and monthly estimate** in one screen
- **Copy and reset controls** for fast payroll communication

## How to use
1. Enter hourly wage.
2. Enter contracted weekly working hours.
3. Enter weekly contracted workdays.
4. Mark attendance status.

## Example
- Hourly wage: 10,030 KRW
- Weekly hours: 20
- Weekly workdays: 5
- Perfect attendance: Yes

→ Paid holiday hours: 4h, weekly holiday pay: about 40,120 KRW

## Notes
- This is a simplified estimate.
- Final payroll is determined by labor contract, attendance logs, and company policy.
- Overtime/night/holiday premiums are outside this calculator’s scope.
