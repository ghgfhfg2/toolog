## Confused about weekly holiday pay? Calculate it in seconds
For hourly workers, weekly holiday pay rules are often confusing.
This tool helps you quickly check **eligibility + estimated weekly/monthly amount**.

## Key features
- Automatic eligibility check (15+ contracted hours/week)
- Auto holiday-hours formula (weekly hours ÷ contracted workdays)
- Legal max holiday hours cap (8h)
- Weekly and monthly estimate shown together

## How to use
1. Enter hourly wage.
2. Enter contracted weekly working hours.
3. Enter contracted workdays per week.
4. Choose attendance status.

## Example
- Hourly wage: 10,030 KRW
- Weekly hours: 20
- Weekly workdays: 5
- Full attendance: Yes

→ Holiday hours: 4h, weekly holiday pay: about 40,120 KRW

## Notes
- This is a quick estimate.
- Actual payroll may differ by contract, attendance records, and company policy.
