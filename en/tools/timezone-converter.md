## Schedule global meetings with confidence
Convert one reference time into a target zone and reduce communication errors across teams, clients, and launch schedules.

The tool uses browser timezone data for DST-aware conversion. The result also shows the target UTC offset and whether the local date stays the same, moves to the previous day, or moves to the next day.

## Why use it
- Compare office hours across regions
- Avoid daylight-saving confusion
- Plan launches and support windows
- Copy a clear converted time into meeting invites

## How to use
1. Choose the source time zone or tap a common pair.
2. Enter the source date and time.
3. Choose the target time zone and check the converted local time.
4. Copy the result for a calendar invite, chat message, or release note.

## Common searches this helps with
- Seoul to New York meeting time
- KST to PST or PDT conversion
- London to Seoul time difference
- DST-safe launch time conversion

## FAQ
### Does this timezone converter handle daylight saving time?
Yes. It uses browser IANA timezone data, so DST is applied for zones and dates where it exists.

### What does the source time zone mean?
The date and time you enter are interpreted as local time in the selected source zone.

### Can I copy the converted meeting time?
Yes. Use the copy result button to paste the converted time into a calendar invite, chat, or email.
