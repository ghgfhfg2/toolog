## What this tool does
Korean Spelling Practice helps you review commonly confused Korean spellings and spacing patterns with short two-choice quiz questions.

## Good for
- quick Korean writing warm-ups
- reviewing confusing everyday expressions
- light practice before writing notices, posts, or messages

## How to use it
1. Choose a quiz category.
2. Pick the number of questions.
3. Start the quiz.
4. Answer each question and review the explanation.
5. Check missed questions at the bottom.
