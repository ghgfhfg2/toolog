## Grocery Budget Checker
Paste your grocery list with estimated prices to see whether it fits your budget. The tool highlights must-buy items, optional items, over-budget amount, and quick cut candidates.

## How to use
1. Enter your grocery budget.
2. Add one item per line with an estimated price.
3. Add words like must, optional, later, or hold when helpful.
4. Run the check and copy the cleaned list.

## FAQ
### Is this an accounting tool?
No. It is a quick planning checker for estimated grocery spending before checkout.

### Is my list uploaded?
No. The list is processed only in your browser.
