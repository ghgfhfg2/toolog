## What this tool does
The Fridge Ingredient Menu Picker helps you turn leftover or everyday ingredients into quick meal ideas.
Instead of searching endless recipes, you can enter what you already have and get a few realistic menu candidates.

## Good for
- using up leftovers before grocery shopping
- deciding dinner after work with limited time
- choosing between rice, soup, toast, noodle, or protein-first directions
- working with the tools you actually have available right now
