## Quick VAT checks for real work
Use this tool when you need to verify:
- VAT amount from net price
- Net and VAT split from gross total

## Supported modes
- **Net-based**: net price → VAT + gross total
- **Gross-based**: gross total → net price + VAT

## How to use
1. Choose net-based or gross-based mode.
2. Enter amount.
3. Confirm net, VAT, and gross values.

## Typical use cases
- Invoice and quotation drafting
- Payment reconciliation
- Sharing clear tax breakdown with partners
