## Why use this tool?
A short group notice can still miss key details: date, time, place, things to bring, reply deadline, and contact method. This tool turns those details into a clear message for group chats, texts, or email.

## How to use
1. Enter the event name, date, time, and place.
2. Add items to bring, reply deadline, or extra notes.
3. Choose a friendly, polite, or reminder tone.
4. Generate the announcement and copy it.

## Good use cases
- First notice for a group chat
- Reminder before the event
- Class, club, family, or online meeting announcements
