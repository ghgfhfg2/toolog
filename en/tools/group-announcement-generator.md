## Why use the Group Announcement Generator?
Group announcements look short, but they often miss something important: date, time, place, things to bring, reply deadline, or who to contact if someone is late. If you keep posting corrections in a group chat, participants can easily get confused.

This tool turns your gathering details into a **notice that is easy to paste directly into a group chat**. You can choose the tone that fits the situation, from a formal notice to a friendly group-chat message or a short reminder.

## How to use it
1. Enter the gathering name, date, and time.
2. Add the place, things to bring, reply deadline, or other important notes.
3. Choose a friendly tone, polite tone, or short reminder tone.
4. Press `Generate announcement` and review the result.
5. Copy it into KakaoTalk, a text message, email, Slack, or any other channel you use.

## Especially useful when
### 1) Posting the first notice in a group chat
You can organize the place and time in one message so everyone sees the same information from the start.

### 2) Sending a reminder the day before a gathering
Restate what has already been shared, then emphasize things to bring or the time people should not miss.

### 3) Writing school, academy, club, or community notices
The tool helps keep the announcement focused on the key details instead of becoming too long or scattered.

## FAQ
### Can I include fees or bank account information?
You can include cost information, but review sensitive details such as bank account numbers or phone numbers before copying, and include them only when necessary.

### Can I include an online meeting link?
Yes. Put the video meeting link or access instructions in the place field, and the tool will include them in the announcement.

### Can I make multiple versions of the same notice?
Yes. Change the tone or purpose and generate again to create versions such as the first notice, reminder, or final deadline notice.
