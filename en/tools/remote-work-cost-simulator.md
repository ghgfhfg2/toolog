## Why compare remote work and commuting costs?
Work-style decisions often feel emotional, but a lot of the difference comes from repeated daily friction:
- transport
- lunch and coffee
- extra home-office spending
- commute time every month

This tool turns those tradeoffs into a quick monthly comparison so you can see whether office days or remote days feel more expensive under your own routine.

## What it compares
Enter:
- total workdays per month
- office days per month
- round-trip commute cost per office day
- lunch and coffee/snack spend per office day
- extra at-home cost per remote day
- daily round-trip commute time
- optional hourly value for your time

The simulator then shows office cost, remote cost, commute-time value, monthly gap, and a simple break-even office-day estimate.

## Useful situations
### Hybrid schedule planning
Test whether 4 office days, 2 office days, or full remote fits your routine better.

### Job or team changes
If two roles differ mostly by working style, this gives you a clearer monthly comparison.

### Time-heavy commutes
Even when transport is cheap, long commute time can change the real picture a lot.
