## Convert area and compare listing prices faster
When listings mix **m²** and **pyeong**, price comparison becomes slow.
This tool converts both ways instantly and adds **price per pyeong** from total price.

## Key features
- Two-way conversion: **m² ↔ pyeong**
- Instant **price-per-pyeong** calculation
- Large-number display support (**10k KRW / 100M KRW**)
- Copy + reset actions for sharing and recalculation

## How to use
1. Enter either area in m² or pyeong.
2. (Optional) Enter total property price.
3. Check converted area and price-per-pyeong outputs.

## Note
Reference factor: **1 pyeong = 3.305785 m²**.
