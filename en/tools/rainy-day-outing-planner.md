## Why use the Rainy Day Outing Planner?
Rainy-day outings can become much more tiring because of small preparation gaps. It is easy to remember an umbrella but end up with soaked shoes, worry about electronics inside your bag, or arrive late because you did not account for slower transit and walking.

This tool is a planner-type tool that uses rain intensity, transport, outing length, shoes, and bag conditions to organize **what to bring before leaving today and how much extra departure time to allow**. It does not replace a weather forecast, but it focuses on turning rain information you already checked into practical preparation steps.

## How to use it
1. Choose the rain intensity and wind level.
2. Select your transport method and expected outing length.
3. Enter today’s shoes, whether your bag is waterproof, and any important belongings.
4. Press `Create preparation plan`.
5. Copy the umbrella, shoes, belongings protection, and departure-buffer checklist and review it before leaving.

## Especially useful when
### 1) Before commuting to work or school during the rainy season
You can quickly check items that are easy to miss repeatedly, such as crowded transit, wet shoes, and spare socks.

### 2) When carrying a laptop or documents
If you write down important belongings, the planner prioritizes protection steps such as a zip bag, waterproof pouch, or bag cover.

### 3) On days when it is easy to be late for an appointment
For heavy rain, wind, public transit, or walking conditions, the planner increases the departure buffer and reflects it in the checklist.

## FAQ
### Do I need to check the weather forecast separately?
Yes. This tool does not call a forecast API. It works best after you check rain intensity and wind in a weather app, then use the planner to convert that information into preparation actions.

### Does it always recommend an umbrella?
For light rain and short indoor-to-indoor movement, it may suggest lighter preparation such as a compact umbrella or raincoat. For heavy rain or wind, it emphasizes a sturdy umbrella, waterproof outerwear, and shoe protection more strongly.

### Can I use it for travel preparation?
It is designed around short outings, but it can also help with same-day trips, concert venue travel, outdoor plans, and other schedules where rain is a key variable.
