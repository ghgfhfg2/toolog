## Split bill calculator for quick group settlement
Use this tool when you need to split a meal, taxi fare, delivery fee, or group expense quickly.

It calculates the base share per person, applies optional rounding, and shows the adjusted amount for the last person so the total still matches exactly.

## Key features
- Split a total amount by number of people
- Optional rounding to 1 / 10 / 100 / 1000 units
- Last-person adjustment for exact reconciliation
- Copy-ready settlement summary
