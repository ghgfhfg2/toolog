## Why use an Interview Follow-up Question Generator?
In many interviews, the first answer matters less than what happens right after it.
A prepared story can sound solid until the interviewer asks for evidence, numbers, ownership, trade-offs, or what you would do differently next time.

This tool helps you rehearse that second layer.
Based on your role, seniority, interview type, and core answer, it generates **five likely follow-up questions plus answer points** so you can spot weak areas before the real interview.

## How to use it
1. Choose your role.
2. Select your seniority and interview type.
3. Paste a short core answer or experience summary.
4. Choose the angle you want to emphasize.
5. Generate the follow-up set and answer each one aloud.

## Good situations for this tool
- Before a mock interview
- When your story sounds fine on paper but feels weak when spoken
- When you want to strengthen metrics, ownership, or collaboration details
