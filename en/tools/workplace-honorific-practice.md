## Why use Workplace Honorific Practice?
Many people struggle less with grammar itself and more with **how polite a sentence should sound in context**.
This tool helps you compare two short expressions and learn which one feels more natural, less abrupt, and easier to use in everyday work communication.

## How to use it
1. Pick a practice range: `Messenger`, `Email`, `Schedule`, or `All`.
2. Choose how many questions you want.
3. Select the more natural polite phrasing.
4. Review the explanation right away and revisit missed items below.

## Good use cases
- Replying in workplace chat without sounding too blunt
- Softening email requests without making them too long
- Practicing polite Korean before interviews, internships, or academic contact
