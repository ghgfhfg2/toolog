## What this tool does
Message Tone Checker reviews work messages and email drafts for direct phrasing, repeated apologies, request clarity, deadline mentions, and overall length.

## Good for
- reviewing Slack, chat, or email drafts before sending
- catching messages that sound too blunt or too vague
- checking whether a request and deadline are stated clearly

## How to use it
1. Paste your message draft.
2. Choose the communication context.
3. Run the check.
4. Review the summary and suggestion notes.
5. Edit your message and send it with more confidence.
