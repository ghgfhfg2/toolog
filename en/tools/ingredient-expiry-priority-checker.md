## Why use this checker?
Fridges often collect ingredients that are close to their consume-by date: tofu, greens, milk, eggs, meat, leftovers, and sauces. This tool helps you decide what to use today, what to plan this week, and what can wait.

## How to use it
1. Enter the ingredient name.
2. Add days left until the consume-by date or your own use-by deadline.
3. Choose storage, package condition, amount, and whether you already have a plan.
4. Review the priority list and practical tips.
