## Why use the Customer Support Message Generator?
When you need a refund, exchange, delivery check, billing correction, or bug report, it can be hard to decide **what details to include and in what order**. Too much emotion can hide the actual request, while a message that is too short often leads to follow-up questions.

This tool turns your product name, issue, desired resolution, order details, and tone into a **clear, polite support message**.

## How to use it
1. Enter the product or service name.
2. Choose the inquiry type.
3. Describe what happened and what resolution you want.
4. Add order details and attachment availability if needed.
5. Copy the result into a support chat, email, or inquiry form.

## Especially useful when
### 1) Requesting a refund or exchange
It keeps the message focused on purchase details, facts, and the requested action.

### 2) Reporting an app or website error
It prompts you to include timing, screenshots, and reproducible details.

### 3) Asking about delayed or missing delivery
It makes the next step—reshipment, refund, or schedule confirmation—clear.

## FAQ
### Can I send the generated text as-is?
Usually yes, but confirm order numbers, dates, attachments, and personal details first.

### Is this for legal dispute letters?
No. This is for ordinary customer support inquiries. For serious disputes, high-value losses, or contract issues, seek appropriate professional or official help.

### Is being aggressive more effective?
Not usually. Clear facts, a concrete request, and a reasonable response deadline are often more useful than anger.
