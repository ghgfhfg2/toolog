## Why use a Meeting Action Item Organizer?
Meetings often end with scattered notes instead of a clean follow-up list. When action items, review points, and simple share notes are mixed together, work slows down after the meeting even if the discussion itself went well.

This tool helps by turning raw notes into a more usable structure.

## How it works
1. Enter a meeting title.
2. Paste notes, one item per line.
3. Choose whether to include owner, priority, due-date, and checkbox formatting.
4. Click organize.
5. Copy the result into chat, docs, or your task tool.

## Useful when
- you need a follow-up message right after a meeting
- you want to separate action items from simple notes
- you plan to move meeting outcomes into Notion, Jira, or Asana
