## Convert hourly wage to monthly and yearly salary quickly
For part-time and hourly contracts, monthly pay changes based on weekly hours, workdays, and weekly holiday pay eligibility.

This tool estimates:
- Weekly gross pay
- Monthly gross pay
- Yearly gross pay

## Formula
1. Base weekly pay = `hourly wage × weekly hours`
2. Weekly holiday pay (optional) = `hourly wage × weekly holiday hours`
3. Monthly pay = `(base weekly + holiday pay) × weeks per month`
4. Yearly pay = `monthly pay × 12`
