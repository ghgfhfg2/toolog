## Why use Recycling Sorting Practice?
Household recycling is easy to do every day, but many items are confusing: receipts, greasy containers, broken glass, chicken bones, coated paper, and small mixed-material packaging.

This tool helps you practice the common categories with short quizzes and instant explanations.

## How to use it
1. Choose a difficulty and range.
2. Read the item shown on screen.
3. Pick the best disposal category.
4. Check the explanation.
5. Repeat missed items to build a more reliable habit.

## Summary
Recycling Sorting Practice is a **learning-style tool for practicing everyday waste sorting decisions**.
