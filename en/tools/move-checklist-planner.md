## Why this move checklist planner helps
Moving becomes stressful when every task stays in your head.
This tool turns the process into a staged checklist so you can see what to do:
- two weeks before
- three days before
- on move day

## What it covers
Based on your move setup, it adjusts emphasis for:
- home type
- moving style
- elevator conditions
- utility / internet transfer
- essential first-day box prep

## Typical use cases
### Studio or one-room move
Useful for small moves where admin and cleanup tasks are easy to forget.

### Family apartment move
Helps split bigger preparation into manageable stages.

### Self-packing move
Especially useful when labeling, sorting, and priority packing matter more.

## FAQ
### Is the checklist fixed?
The core structure is fixed, but highlighted tasks change based on your selections.

### Is it a replacement for a professional moving service checklist?
No. It is a practical planning draft, and you should still confirm service-specific requirements with your mover or building manager.
