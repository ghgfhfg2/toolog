## Why use it?
Search ranking can be harmed by repetitive spam wording, forced marketing tone, or unsafe outbound patterns. This checker helps you catch those signals before posting.

## What it checks
- Spam/promo-heavy keywords
- Overclaim terms in health/legal/finance contexts
- Contact IDs, phone numbers, and suspicious links
- Excessive hashtag or repeated phrase patterns

## How to use
1. Paste your draft text.
2. Review highlighted risky terms and patterns.
3. Rewrite naturally with clearer context and useful information.
