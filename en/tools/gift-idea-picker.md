## Why use a Gift Idea Picker?
Choosing a gift is usually harder than paying for it.
For birthdays, thank-you gifts, anniversaries, and small celebrations, the problem is often not finding *something* to buy — it is narrowing down **what fits this person and this moment**.

Instead of scrolling through endless shopping results, this tool helps you narrow the direction first.
It uses:
- who the gift is for
- the occasion
- your budget
- the mood you want
- how much time you have to prepare

to recommend **five gift ideas that are easier to choose right now**.

## How to use it
1. Choose the type of recipient.
2. Select the occasion: birthday, thank-you, anniversary, celebration, or seasonal gift.
3. Set the budget range and overall gift vibe.
4. Choose whether you need it today, within a few days, or can plan ahead.
5. If needed, exclude food gifts or prioritize easy-to-wrap options.
6. Review the suggested ideas and use them to decide what to buy or share.

## Especially useful in these cases
### 1) When you need to choose a birthday gift quickly
If it has to be sent today, fast-delivery picks like gift cards or compact practical items usually work better.

### 2) When you want a thank-you gift that feels polite but not too heavy
It fits situations like coworkers, teachers, or business contacts where **good manners matter but too much weight feels awkward**.

### 3) When you are stuck between sentimental and practical anniversary gifts
Once you decide whether warmth, usefulness, or a more premium feel matters most, the candidate list gets much easier to narrow.

## FAQ
### Can I still use it if I do not know the person’s taste very well?
Yes. That is when it helps most. Even without knowing exact preferences, you can first narrow toward safe directions like practical, light thank-you, or instant-delivery options.

### Does it always show exactly five ideas?
Usually yes, but if your conditions are very narrow, some candidates may drop out. If that happens, widening the budget or vibe slightly will give you more variety.

### Does it recommend specific brands or shopping links?
Not in this version. The focus is on **gift categories and why they fit**, so you can decide faster before shopping.
