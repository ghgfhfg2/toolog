## Why use a Gift Idea Picker?
Gift shopping usually gets stuck before the purchase step.
The hard part is narrowing the direction: practical or cozy, light or polished, quick or planned.

This tool helps you turn that vague 고민 into a short list you can act on.

## How to use it
1. Choose who the gift is for.
2. Pick the occasion.
3. Set your budget and desired vibe.
4. Choose whether you need it fast or can plan ahead.
5. Review the 5 suggested ideas and reasons.

## Useful when
- you need a birthday gift quickly
- you want a polite but not too heavy thank-you gift
- you are choosing between practical and sentimental gift directions

## Summary
The Gift Idea Picker is a **picker-type tool that helps narrow fitting gift directions by relationship, occasion, budget, vibe, and prep timing**.
