## What this tool does
Use this simulator before booking a movie ticket. Add up to four seat candidates, rate screen distance, center position, aisle access, group size, and your viewing preference, then compare the recommended order.

## How to use it
1. Enter candidate seats such as G8, H center, or J aisle.
2. Choose distance, center position, aisle access, and group size.
3. Pick what matters most: immersion, neck comfort, or easy exit.
4. Review the score and reason before booking.

## FAQ
### Does it read a real seat map?
No. You manually enter the seats you are considering.

### Is the top score always the right seat?
No. Treat it as a practical comparison based on common viewing preferences.
