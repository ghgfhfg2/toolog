## Why use Household Chore Picker?
When families, roommates, or partners share housework, the hardest part is often not the work itself but deciding who does what without making it feel unfair.

This tool helps when:

- dishes, trash, or cleaning keeps landing on the same person
- heavy chores such as bathroom cleaning are easy to postpone
- you do not want to renegotiate the chore list every week
- you need a quick checklist to paste into a group chat

It keeps a bit of randomness while helping avoid one person receiving all the tasks.

## How to use it
1. Enter people, one name per line.
2. Enter chores, one task per line.
3. Choose balanced, random, or rotation mode.
4. Decide whether to spread heavy chores and use checklist formatting.
5. Copy the result into your family or roommate chat.

## Summary
Household Chore Picker is a **picker-type tool** for turning people and chore lists into a quick assignment chart without relying on another calculator-style interaction.
